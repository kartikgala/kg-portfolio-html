Project: CLT_2022

CO-LIVING IN THE BALLARD ESTATE PRECINCT: The residential floors are inspired by the traditional wooden homes in India as well as detailing of the Malad Stone Office buildings in the precinct. The rooms are stepped, cantilevered providing necessary shade from the harsh sun and rain in our tropical climate. The staggering plan of the rooms forms an association with the ashlar stone courses.
