Kartik Gala

An Architect with a major in Advanced Architecture. After studying Architecture at Kamla Raheja Vidyanidhi Institute for Architecture (KRVIA) - Mumbai, India, he furthered his Architectural education through researching and developing interdisciplinary skills at IAAC (Institute for Advanced architecture of Catalonia) - Barcelona, Spain. He excels in Advanced Architecture, Digital Fabrication and Advanced Manufacturing Technologies such as 3D Printing, CNC Fabrication, Blade / Laser Cutting & Material research.

He has been Teaching Architecture for 10 years to Undergraduate and Master’s level students at various colleges such as Balwant School OF Architecture (BSSA), KRVIA, IES, SEA, Academy Of Architecture (AOA), Pillai’s, Ecole Intuit Lab - Mumbai & JBS - Hyderabad. These Studios explored Architecture, Interior Architecture, Game Design, Interaction Design, Product Design & allied fields.

A Hobbyist, Tinkerer and lover of Technology & Art, he is exploring Anamorphic Art, Projection Mapping, Paper Engineering & Interaction Design through various mediums such as music events, art shows & workshops. A Mumbai based independent studio, where he and his team practises Architecture, Interior Architecture, Product Design, Creative Coding, Interaction Design and is setting up an applied innovation design lab with independent collaborators.
